Diagnostic Imaging

Magnetic Resonance Imaging is a relative new imaging study, which is also widely available in hospitals and outpatient radiology facilities. An MR unit is very similar in appearance to a CT or PET scanner, although there are some differences. MR does not use radiation, and it allows superior images of soft tissue structures, such as the neck.

Emergency Services

eMobilis Clinic operates a 24-hour state-of-the-art medical dispatch center staffed with EMT-Intermediates, EMT-Paramedics and EMD (Emergency Medical Dispatcher) trained personnel. The center serves as the nucleus of the EMS operation, receiving calls from local 9-1-1 public safety-answering points, hospitals and other contract accounts, as well as private requests for transportation.
